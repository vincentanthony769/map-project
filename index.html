Mapping Mariah Carey's Merry Christmas Monarchy

The holiday anthem ranked #1 on Apple Music in over 60 countries worldwide

December 16, 2023

Photo Credit: Vincent Anthony

By Vincent Anthony

Mariah Carey’s perennial hit, “All I Want For Christmas Is You,” has become a worldwide phenomenon. This holiday season alone, the track reigns at #1 on Billboard’s Global 200 for the last two weeks and is expected to notch its third, in addition to reclaiming the pole position on the Hot 100, on Monday.

>With the rise in popularity of music streaming services worldwide, the song continues to gain more and more streams each year. The Apple Music charts, which rank songs overall and by genre in 167 countries, paint a picture of the song’s global dominance. Historically, the song has peaked at #1 in 67 countries and has peaked at #1 on the Holiday genre chart in all but one.
